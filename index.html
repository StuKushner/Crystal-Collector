Crystal Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a number of points to your total score.

If your total score is equal to the random number, you win. If not, you lose.

The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

When you play the game again, the random number will be reset.

When you're ready, click on a crystal to start playing!
